The approach

Allergen & health first.

Every menu starts from what our partner kitchens already do best — vegan, gluten-free, allergen-conscious, and health conscious cooking that was never an asterisk on someone else's menu. We pair that with real space design, so the room feels as considered as the plate. Same curation instinct behind 333's everyday partners, applied to the room you'll remember.

Curated Food Ambience

The room, set like the menu.

Table settings, styling, and staging curated with the same care as the food itself. Optional add-on for hosts who want the space designed alongside the menu, not just the plate. We also have top local spaces we have well established relationships with.

Optional Sound Ambience

Sound, curated like the menu.

Additional frequency curation option, hifi analog sound set ups and proper acoustic and speaker mapping, playlist perfection on lossless or vinyl or both. Possible availability from our small network of some of the top indie bands and musicians.

Wellness Add-On

Wellness, curated like the menu.

Not a separate offering — an extension of the same meal. NA & mocktail curation alongside the drink program, wellness gift partnerships from our sponsor network, and custom add-on class options. Perfect before a dinner, at a festival, or during a wedding weekend.
